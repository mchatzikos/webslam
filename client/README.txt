Installing
    * Place the language file (en-GB.com_hello.ini) into /{your hub install}/languages/en-GB/
    * Place the contents of this component in /{your hub install}/components/com_hello/
    * Place the manifest (hello.xml) in /{your hub install}/administrator/components/com_hello/
        ! Unless creating an administrator interface for your component, no other fiels are needed
        * Log in to your administrator area (http://yourhub.org/administrator)
        * Find "Extensions" in the main menu
        * Select "Extensions Manager"
        * Click "Discover" in the sub-menu
        * Click "Discover" in the toolbar
        * Find the hello component in the resulting list and click the checkbox
        * Click "Install" in the toolbar
